Vector Basis Finder

Enter a set of m vectors in ℝn to determine the basis of their span.
This tool calculates both the Subset Basis (linearly independent columns from your input) and the Reduced Basis (simplest unique form).

Dimension (Rank):

1. Subset Basis

Linearly independent vectors selected from your input.

2. Reduced Basis (RREF)

Simplest basis for the column space (often Identity).
